Scope

Alma works with physiological signals to infer patterns of cadence and coherence.

Alma offers interpretive cues, not conclusions.

Alma supports self-regulation and awareness, not decision-making on behalf of the user.

Boundaries

Alma does not diagnose, predict mental health conditions, or replace professional care.

Alma does not optimize productivity, performance, or behavioral compliance.

Alma does not claim objective truth about emotions — only contextual resonance.

Design principle (1–2 rânduri)

When uncertainty appears, Alma chooses restraint over assertion.
